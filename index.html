School Achievements

Top 5% in Texas
Coppell Middle West placed in the top 5% of all schools in Texas

Coppell Middle West placed in the top 5% of all schools in Texas for overall test scores (math proficiency is top 5%, and reading proficiency is top 5%) for the 2020-21 school year. The percentage of students achieving proficiency in math is 76% (which is higher than the Texas state average of 37%) for the 2020-21 school year.

What's New

  • New Building For The School

    Coppell Middle School West has recently received a new building, which is a welcome addition to the campus. The new building is a modern and spacious facility that provides state-of-the-art classrooms and facilities for students and faculty. The building has been designed with the latest technology in mind, including interactive whiteboards, high-speed internet connectivity, and advanced audio-visual equipment. The classrooms are well-lit and spacious, providing a comfortable learning environment for students. In addition to the classrooms, the building also houses a library, a computer lab, and a cafeteria. Overall, the new building is a great asset to the school, and it will undoubtedly enhance the learning experience of students and the teaching experience of faculty.
